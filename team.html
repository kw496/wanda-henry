Young Wang

Dr. Young Wang’s career pathways include the clinical laboratory, in-vitro diagnostic and biopharmaceutical industries. He is familiar with GCP/GLP regulation and CAP/CLIA/ISO guidance, thereby merging his academic background and industry experience, and bringing them to the next level. He has been working in the field of lab accreditation, quality management, in-vitro diagnostic products and regulatory submission for over 20 years.

Afshin Safaie

Dr. Afshin Safaie is a medical laboratory scientist with 20+ years of experience in public health and clinical research. His experience ranges from serving as a technical supervisor in field labs to managing public health labs for the CDC in Iran. He has served as a director for the Reference Health Laboratory and a WHO collaboration center. He earned his Doctoral Degree in Laboratory Science and his Master’s Degree in Public Health (MPH).
